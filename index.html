02 · Thesis02 · 论题

The No-Oracle Thesis无预言机论题

The current debate surrounding AI consciousness — whether approached through computational functionalism or its physicalist critiques — has reached an epistemological deadlock.围绕 AI 意识的当前争论——无论是从计算功能主义出发，还是从物理主义批评出发——都已经进入一个认识论僵局。

The struggle stems from a hidden shared premise: both sides are, in different ways, searching for a “Yes-Oracle.” One side looks for an emergent syntactic property. The other looks for a privileged biological substrate. Both hope for something that could definitively output YES: subjective experience is instantiated.这个僵局来自一个隐藏的共同前提：双方都在以不同方式寻找某种 “YES-Oracle”。一边寻找某种涌现的句法性质，另一边寻找某种特权化的生物基底。双方都希望找到一个能够最终输出 YES 的认证者：主观体验已经被实例化。

But this framing may be fundamentally flawed.但这个设问本身可能就是错的。

An AGI, no matter how vast its parameter count, can no more natively verify whether it is raining in London at 6:37 AM than a simple smart contract can. Both operate within computational closure. Both require an external, physics-bound interface — an oracle — to attest to events outside the system.一个 AGI，无论参数规模多大，都不能比一个简单智能合约更原生地验证伦敦早上 6:37 是否正在下雨。二者都处在计算闭包之中；二者都需要一个外部的、受物理约束的接口——一个预言机——去证明系统之外的事件。

The inability of a digital system to self-certify physical reality is not a bug of its complexity. It is a consequence of its syntax. The analogy is not about equating inner experience with external weather. It targets the bridge claim: the move from syntactic trajectory to mental instantiation.数字系统不能自证物理现实，并不是复杂度不足的 bug，而是句法系统本身的后果。这个类比不是把内部体验等同于外部天气。它针对的是那个桥接声明：从句法轨迹跳到心智实例化。

The No-Oracle problem asks a narrower and sharper question: not whether this framework can prove or disprove consciousness itself, but whether a claim that an AI instantiates consciousness has admissible evidential standing.无预言机问题提出的是一个更窄也更锋利的问题：不是这个框架能否证明或否定意识本身，而是一个“AI 实例化了意识”的声明，是否具备可采纳的证据资格。

If an architecture cannot specify how it escapes its own alignability closure through an admissible external or physical interface Π, then any bridge from syntactic isomorphism to mental isomorphism remains evidentially inadmissible.如果一个架构无法说明它如何通过一个可采纳的外部或物理接口 Π 逃出自身的 alignability closure，那么任何从句法同构桥接到心智同构的声明，都仍然是证据上不可采纳的。

This does not deny the metaphysical possibility of AI consciousness. It distinguishes possibility from admissible assertion. A claim may remain possible while lacking evidential standing. The No-Oracle problem concerns the latter.这并不否认 AI 意识的形而上可能性。它区分的是可能性与可采纳声明。一个声明可以仍然可能，但缺少证据资格。无预言机问题关心的是后者。

Both sides are searching for a Yes-Oracle.双方都在寻找某种 YES-Oracle。
The disputed object is not the report. It is the bridge.真正有争议的不是报告，而是桥。
Possibility is not admissible assertion.可能性不等于可采纳声明。

01 · ORIGIN

Origin · The place this question came from.Origin · 起点

My research did not originate in epistemological philosophy. It came from fifteen years of working around heavy off-road platforms, offline / off-grid operations, and the definition of industrial-grade user needs in real physical wilderness.我的研究不来自于认识论哲学，而是从十五年的重型越野平台、离线/离网作业和在真实的物理荒野中定义工业级别用户需求中获得的灵感。

Since 2005, I joined one of China’s earliest high-mobility special-purpose off-road platform projects benchmarked against the Unimog class, and spent fifteen years in that product line. The only reason such modular platforms exist is to enter places that centralized infrastructure cannot cover — long-distance oil and gas pipelines, remote power-line inspection, borderland no-man’s zones, emergency disconnection zones, and agricultural operations under extreme environments. I was not the frontline engineer drawing blueprints. My role was to define this space: scenario segmentation, requirement decomposition, platform-boundary judgment, and repeated conversations with industry users in the mud.2005 年起，我加入了中国最早一批对标乌尼莫克（Unimog）级别的高机动特种越野平台项目，并在这条产品线里深耕了十五年。这种模块化平台存在的唯一意义，就是挺进那些中心化基础设施覆盖不到的地方——油气长输管线、远程电力巡检、边境无人区、应急断网地带以及极端环境下的农业作业。我不是画图纸的一线工程师，我做的是定义这片空间的事：场景切分、需求解构、平台边界判定，以及与各行业用户在泥泞中反复对话。

02 · VANTAGE

Vantage · What that position revealed.Vantage · 那个位置给我的视角

That position gave me a perspective most digitally native researchers rarely have: the space where humans create physical value is far larger than what centralized trust infrastructure can cover.这个位置给了我一个绝大多数数字原生研究者很难获得的视角：人类创造物理价值的空间，远比中心化信任基础设施能够覆盖的范围大得多。

In today’s digital world, there is often no independent mechanism to attest that work performed in these “dark zones” actually happened: a rescue vehicle completing emergency work in a disconnected canyon, an unmanned device finishing a precision operation deep in the desert, or farmers trying to prove that their products meet a certain quality standard. These are real acts of labor and production. Yet what they can submit to digital systems is often only self-reported logs, coordinates, photos, and paperwork. The conclusion I reached was simple and severe:在今天的数字世界里，往往没有独立机制能够证明那些发生在“信任暗区”的作业确实存在：一辆抢险车在断网峡谷中完成救援，一台无人设备在沙漠深处完成精准作业，或者农户试图证明自己的农产品达到了某种质量标准。这些都是真实发生的劳动和生产活动。但它们能够提交给数字系统的，通常只有自报的日志、坐标、照片和文书。我的结论很简单，也很残酷：

In the physical world, no attestation = no transactional value.
Because of this trust fracture, a massive amount of physical value remains locked outside the boundary of current infrastructure — and outside the automated processes that increasingly allocate capital, credit, insurance, and market access. It cannot be reliably converted into social benefit.在物理世界，无证明 = 无交易价值。
正是因为这层信任断裂，世界上大量物理价值被锁在现有基础设施边界之外，也被排除在越来越多自动化分配资本、信贷、保险和市场准入的流程之外，难以可靠地转化为社会收益。

03 · CONVERGENCE

Convergence · Where the epistemic gap appeared.Convergence · 与认识论缝隙的相遇

Interestingly, when I tried to find an underlying architectural solution for this kind of “physical entitlement in the wilderness,” I unexpectedly ran into an ultimate epistemological gap. I realized that a machine in the Gobi reporting to a server, “I completed the inspection,” and today’s large language model claiming to humans, “I have generated consciousness,” are structurally isomorphic at the bottom: both are issuing a claim about some ontological process or existence related to themselves and the world, and both are records that cannot be self-verified within their own “symbolic closure.”有趣的是，当我试图为这种”荒野中的物理确权”寻找底层架构解法时，我意外地撞上了一个终极的认识论缝隙。我发现，一台戈壁滩上的机器向服务器回传”我完成了巡检”，与今天的大语言模型向人类声称”我产生了意识”，在问题的底层结构上是同构的——它们都是在发出一个关于他们对世界的某种本体过程/存在的声明，都是一份无法在自身”符号闭包”之内被自我验证。

At that moment I realized that what I was facing was no longer merely an AI philosophy problem, but a massive system-engineering blind spot urgently requiring entitlement.那一刻我意识到，我面对的不再仅仅是一个 AI 哲学问题，而是一个亟待确权的巨大系统工程盲区。

04 · THE SHIFT · Agentic AI / machine autonomy / RWA04 · THE SHIFT · AGENTIC AI / 机器自治 / RWA 时代的信任基座

The Shift · The problem of the age.The Shift · 时代命题

If a mechanism can be built to break this symbolic closure, allowing remote physical operations to be independently verified and independently entitled, it will trigger an assetization wave far beyond any previous era. When Agentic AI, machine autonomy, and RWA become irreversible futures, this mechanism will move the foundation of “trust” down from fragile centralized clearing layers into a hard physical evidence-interface layer.如果能构建一种打破这种符号闭包的机制，让远方的物理作业能够被独立验证、独立确权，它将引发一场远超以往任何时代的资产化浪潮。当 Agentic AI、机器自治与 RWA（真实世界资产）成为不可逆的未来，这套机制将把”信任”的基座，从脆弱的中心化清算层，直接下沉到坚硬的物理证据接口层。
